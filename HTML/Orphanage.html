OUR GROUP In the heart of every child is a hunger for home. Not just for food and a place to sleep, but for safety and community.

We are a family, where love guides us all as we grow and expand year after year. Not just a regular children's home, we are a nearly self-sustaining organization; a working farm, a vibrant school, receiving no government assistance of any kind.
